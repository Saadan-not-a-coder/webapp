PROJECT: Online Quiz Portal - Group Milestone 1
------------------------------------------------------------------

=== WORKFLOW 1: TEACHER (Quiz Creation) ===

Role: Teacher
Goal: Design and publish assessments for students.
Key Features: Question Bank, Timer Settings, Difficulty Levels.
Steps:
1. Teacher logs in and clicks "Create New Quiz" on the dashboard.
2. Enters quiz details: Title, Description, Duration (e.g., 30 mins), and Total Marks.
3. Selects "Add Question" to input text, multiple-choice options, and mark the correct answer.
4. Configures availability settings (e.g., Randomization of questions).
5. Clicks "Publish" to make the quiz visible to enrolled students.
------------------------------------------------------------------

=== WORKFLOW 2: STUDENT (Quiz Attempt) ===

Role: Student
Goal: Attempt the assigned quiz securely.
Key Features: Real-time Timer, Auto-save, Instant Submission.
Steps:
1. Student logs in and sees the "Upcoming Quizzes" list posted by the Teacher.
2. Clicks "Start Quiz" (System verifies if the current time is within the allowed window).
3. Answers questions one by one; the system auto-saves progress to prevent data loss.
4. Monitors the countdown timer displayed at the top of the screen.
5. Clicks "Submit" to finish the attempt (or system auto-submits when time expires).
------------------------------------------------------------------

=== WORKFLOW 3: TEACHER (Analytics & Grading) ===

Role: Teacher
Goal: Monitor student performance and finalize grades.
Key Features: Gradebook, CSV Export, Performance Graphs.
Steps:
1. Teacher navigates to the "Results" tab after the quiz deadline has passed.
2. Views the "Class Performance" graph (Average Score, Highest Score, Failure Rate).
3. Clicks on a specific student's name to review their individual answers.
4. Manually adjusts marks if necessary (e.g., for disputed questions).
5. Clicks "Export Grades" to download the final results as a CSV file.
